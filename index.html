🇬🇧 UK Age Appropriate Design Code
United Kingdom · Children's Commissioner
Requires digital services likely accessed by children to provide privacy protections by default. Applies to apps, games, websites, and connected toys. Mandates age assurance mechanisms and restricts data profiling of under-18s.
ACTIVE230+ platforms affected
🇺🇸 COPPA 2.0
United States · FTC Enforcement
Major update expanding protections to teenagers under 17. Prohibits targeted advertising to minors and introduces new consent requirements. FTC enforcement actions escalating in 2024–2025 with significant fines.
ACTIVEMajor enforcement wave
🇪🇺 EU Digital Services Act — Minors
European Union · All 27 member states
DSA requires very large online platforms to assess and mitigate risks to minors. Age verification and parental controls increasingly required. Non-compliance fines up to 6% of global turnover.
IMPLEMENTATIONAll EU market platforms
🇦🇺 Australia Online Safety Act
Australia · eSafety Commissioner
Australia's landmark social media ban for under-16s passed Nov 2024. Platforms must take reasonable steps to verify age. Up to AUD $50M in fines. Sets a global precedent — APAC markets watching closely.
⚡ INCOMINGAll social / gaming platforms
🇺🇸 Texas SCOPE Act
Texas, USA · State-level (15+ state wave)
Requires parental consent for minors to use social media platforms. Age verification via government ID or third-party systems mandated. Part of a wave — 15+ US states have similar bills in progress.
⚡ INCOMINGUS state-level wave
🌏 APAC Youth Safety Wave
South Korea · Singapore · India · Others
Multiple APAC markets introducing age verification requirements following Australia's lead. South Korea's PIPA, Singapore's Online Safety Act, and India's DPDP Act all contain youth provisions requiring compliance infrastructure.
